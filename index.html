Easy Peasy MAGGIE Noodles Recipe

Recipe Varients


There are days when we get lazy and that is when we create thinks like maggie noddles Easy-Peasy. But , if you think we compromised on this one, you are wrong - you can never go wrong with your favourite MAGGIE Noddles because there is #NothingLikeMaggie! it's amazing how tomatoes, oniens, green peas and some cuminseeds, can make so much diffrence and the best part is-- it's as easy as one, two, three

Ingredients

For 2 Servings
  • 1 Packet - MAGGIE 2- Minute Noodles ( masala )
  • 4 Tablespoon - Maggie Rich Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 Tablespoon - Oil
  • 1 teaspoon - Cumin Seeds
  • 2 - Tomato ( finely Chopped )
  • 1 - Onion ( Finely Chopped )
  • 1 Cup - Peas
  • 1 Pinch - Salt

Instructions


Step 1

Prepare Ingredients as mentioned in the list

Step 2

Heat the oil and then roast the cumin seeds in it. Then, toss in the tomatoes, the onions and cook them well. Add the peas and the MAGGI Tomato ketchup, give it a stir, and let them cook for a while!

Step 3

Cook one pack of MAGGI Masala Noodles (just follow the instructions on the pack!) and transfer it onto a dish.

Step 4

Put the cumin-tomato-onion mix right on top of the MAGGI noodles and you are ready to serve the easy peasy fun!
